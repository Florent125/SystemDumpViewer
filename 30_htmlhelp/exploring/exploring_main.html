Main Screen

Once you have opened the Systemdump you will see the main screen with the overview just like the one in the browser. You now can change the section by selecting it from the drop-down menu on the top of the window, or by clicking into the desired part of the overview. The sections "Logger" and "Profiler" can only be accessed over the drop-down.
